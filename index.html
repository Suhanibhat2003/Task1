The SFRI group originated from discussions among a few like-minded students at IISc Bangalore in 2016, driven by a shared vision to improve science education in rural India. Recognizing that children in rural areas often lack access to quality science education, which limits their opportunities and potential, these students felt compelled to take action. Today, the group has expanded to include students, staff, and faculty from various departments across the campus. United by this common goal, we are dedicated to making science accessible and engaging for rural children, empowering them to explore, learn, and ultimately transform their futures.
